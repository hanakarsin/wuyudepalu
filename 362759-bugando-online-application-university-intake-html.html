The institution's core mandate is to train and prepare students to be healthcare professionals. To join the institution, complete the Bugando University online application process and await feedback.

Through the CUHAS Bugando online application system, you can enroll for diploma, bachelor's, master's, and PhD programs. Since each course has specific entry requirements, ensure you meet the minimum requirements before proceeding with your application.

Bugando University online application guide

Once you have the necessary academic qualifications and the relevant supporting documents needed by the institution, follow this CUHAS Bugando online application process:

  • Select the program category and ensure applications are still open.
  • Read instructions on how to pay the application fee.
  • Click "Go to step two."
  • Fill out the "Education Certification(s) Verifications" section.
  • Complete the "Login Information" section.
  • Click "Create account" to create an account on the CUHAS OSIM (student management system).
  • Follow the prompts displayed on your account to complete the application.
  • Ensure to provide all the relevant documents before submitting your information.
  • Before submitting your application, you will encounter the fee Payment Verification section, which you must complete. You can only submit your application after completing that step.

The Bugando University fees are payable in full at the beginning of each academic year, in two installments at the beginning of each semester, or four equal installments. All payments must be made using CONTROL NUMBERS.

Failure to pay the required fees by the end of week two of the second semester attracts a penalty of Tshs. 50,000. The fourth and final installment must be paid two weeks before/or before the examination—otherwise, another penalty of Tshs. 50,000 will be charged.

What is the application fee for CUHAS?

Bugando University applicants pay a non-refundable application fee of Tshs. 50,000 using a CONTROL NUMBER generated from the CUHAS OSIM (student management system). Each applicant gets a unique number/code.

How to pay the application fee for CUHAS?

For Vodacom (M-PESA) users (national applicants):

  • Dial *150*00#.
  • Enter 1 (Send money).
  • Enter 4 (To bank).
  • Enter 1 (CRDB).
  • Enter 1 (Enter Reference).
  • Enter your reference number—the CONTROL NUMBER generated by the CUHAS OSIM (student management system).
  • Enter the amount.
  • Enter PIN.
  • Enter 1 (to Confirm).
  • You will receive a message confirming your payment to Bugando University.

Diploma in Medical Laboratory Sciences (DMLS)

You should have a Certificate of Secondary Education (CSEE) with five passes, including a “D” pass in Chemistry, Biology, Physics, Basic Mathematics, and English Language.

You need a Certificate of Secondary Education (CSEE) with five passes, including a “D” pass in Chemistry, Biology, Physics, Basic Mathematics, and English Language.

CUHAS Bugando undergraduate courses

Undergraduate courses offered at Bugando University and their entry requirements include:

Doctor of Medicine

Direct applicants should have three principal passes in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology with a minimum of 6 points: A minimum of D grade in Chemistry, Biology, and Physics.

Equivalent applicants should have a Diploma in Clinical Medicine with an average of “B" or a minimum GPA of 3.0. In addition, an applicant must have a minimum of “D” grades in Mathematics, Chemistry, Biology, Physics, and English at O-level.

Bachelor of Medical Laboratory Sciences

Equivalent applicants should have a Diploma in Medical Laboratory Sciences with an average of “B" or a minimum GPA of 3.0. Furthermore, applicants must have a minimum of “D” grades in Mathematics, Chemistry, Biology, Physics, and English at O-level.

Bachelor of Science in Medical Imaging and Radiotherapy

Direct applicants should have three principal passes in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology with a minimum of 6 points: A minimum of D grade in Chemistry, Biology, and Physics.

Equivalent applicants should have a Diploma in Medical Imaging or Radiography with an average of "B" or a minimum GPA of 3.0. The applicant must also have a minimum of "D" grades in Mathematics, Chemistry, Biology, Physics, and English at O-level.

Postgraduate courses offered at CUHAS Bugando

The institution offers the following postgraduate courses:

Master of Medicine (MMED)

The school offers MMED programs in the clinical disciplines of Internal Medicine, Surgery, Obstetrics and Gynecology, Anatomical Pathology, Pediatrics, and Anesthesiology. The minimum entry requirements for MMED programs are:

  • A Medical Doctor degree or equivalent from a recognized higher learning institution.
  • A grade of B or higher in your undergraduate final exams in the specialty you want to study.
  • Completed internship and obtained a grade of B or higher in the specialty you want to study.
  • Completed at least a year as a medical practitioner in a recognized hospital.

Master of Public Health (MPH)

The minimum entry requirements for MPH programs are:

  • A Medical Doctor degree or equivalent with a grade B or above in Community Medicine and at least one year of working experience.
  • A B.Sc. Nursing degree with a grade B or above in Community Medicine and at least one year of working experience.
  • A first degree in health-related disciplines (Health officers, Environmental health, Pharmacy, or Laboratory sciences) is acceptable, provided the student passes with a lower second and has at least one year of working experience.
  • A Master's Degree in health-related disciplines with at least one year of working experience.

Master in Pediatrics Nursing (Msc.PN)

The minimum entry requirements for Msc.PN programs are:

  • A GPA of at least 2.7 in Bachelor of Science in Nursing (BScN)/Bachelor of Science in Nursing Education (BScNE)/Bachelor of Science in Mental Health Nursing (BScM)/Bachelor of Science in Midwifery (BScMid)/Bachelor of Science in Nursing Management from a recognized University
  • Graduates from other recognized Universities should have at least a 2.7 GPA in their first degrees. A minimum of two years of work experience is also required.
  • Before enrolling in the program, graduates should have a valid license to practice as registered nurses and midwives with the Tanzania Nurses and Midwives Council (TNMC).

Master of Science in Clinical Microbiology and Diagnostic Molecular Biology

The minimum entry requirements for these programs are:

  • A first degree in Medical Laboratory Sciences or its equivalent with an overall GPA not less than 2.7
  • A first degree in health sciences with at least a 2.7 overall GPA or a B for unclassified degrees provided they passed with at least B or above in Microbiology/Immunology, Molecular Biology or Biochemistry, or cell biology with at least 200hrs.
  • A Bachelor of Science degree in Biomedical Sciences (microbiology, molecular biology, immunology, parasitology, or Bachelor in Laboratory Sciences) with an overall GPA not less than 2.7, provided they passed with at least a B grade or above in Microbiology/Immunology, Molecular Biology, Biochemistry, or Cell Biology.

Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) programs

The minimum entry requirements for Bugando University's PhD programs are:

  • The prospective candidate must demonstrate the capacity to carry out research independently and the ability to pursue the proposed study program.
  • A candidate for admission for a PhD must be a holder of a relevant Master's Degree (MSc or MMED) of CUHAS-Bugando or any other recognized institution of higher learning within or outside Tanzania.

Bugando University hostels

Students’ accommodation is available on campus (MD Hostel for undergraduates and SHAH House for postgraduates). Diploma students can be accommodated in the hostels run by the BMC.

The MD Hostel rooms accommodate two students, with shared wash-facilities and communal areas on the ground floor. The SHAH House provides self-catered facilities within each apartment.

Where is CUHAS Bugando?

Bugando University, also known as the Catholic University of Health and Allied Sciences (CUHAS Bugando), is located in Mwanza, Tanzania, along the Wurzburg road.

What is the PO Box for Bugando University?

The postal address for the Catholic University of Health and Allied Sciences (CUHAS Bugando) is CUHAS, P.O. Box 1464 Mwanza, Tanzania.

What courses are offered at CUHAS Bugando?

Bugando University offers public health-related diplomas, undergraduate degrees, and postgraduate programs (masters and PhDs).

What are the entry requirements for the CUHAS Bugando diploma?

You need a Certificate of Secondary Education (CSEE) with five passes, including a "D" pass in Chemistry, Biology, Physics, Basic Mathematics, and English Language, to study for a diploma at the Catholic University of Health and Allied Sciences (CUHAS Bugando).

What are the entry requirements for the CUHAS Bugando degree?

Direct undergraduate degree applicants need three principal passes in Chemistry, Biology, and either Physics or Advanced Mathematics or Nutrition with a minimum of 6 points: a minimum of grade C in Chemistry, grade D in Biology, and grade E in Physics/Advanced Mathematics/Nutrition.

Does CUHAS Bugando have accommodation?

Students' accommodation is available on campus, but since there is limited housing, priority is usually given to first-year students. Those who miss on-campus rooms can seek alternative accommodation off-campus at nearby private hostels and private rental homes.

Where are the CUHAS Bugando campuses?

The Catholic University of Health and Allied Sciences - Bugando is located at Bugando Hill, within the Bugando Medical Centre (BMC) premises in Mwanza.
